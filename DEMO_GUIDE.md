**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: No! You can use guest mode. But creating an account lets you sync progress across devices.

**Q: Is it really free?**
A: Yes, completely free. No ads, no premium features, no hidden costs.

**Q: What if I don't have a microphone?**
A: You can skip speaking mode, but we recommend trying it - it really helps!

**Q: Can I go back to previous scenes?**
A: Currently, you progress forward through scenes. We may add scene selection in future updates.

**Q: What happens if I close the browser mid-scene?**
A: Your XP and stats are saved. You can continue from where you left off.

**Q: How is my pronunciation scored?**
A: The app uses speech recognition to compare your speech to the expected sentence and calculates a similarity score.

A: Guest users can clear browser data. Signed-in users can logout (clears local) or contact support for cloud reset.

**Q: Will more stories be added?**
A: Yes! We plan to add more stories across all difficulty levels.

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: Chrome, Edge, Safari, Firefox (latest versions). Chrome recommended for best speech recognition.

**Q: Can I use this offline?**
A: Currently requires internet for video streaming and cloud sync. Offline mode planned for future.
