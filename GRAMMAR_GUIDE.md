## Key Grammar Themes Throughout

### 1. Contractions (7 scenes)
- should've, won't, don't, you're, we're, wasn't, gonna
- **Teaching Point:** Contractions are essential in natural spoken English

### 2. Modal Verbs (3 scenes)
- should've (modal perfect)
- won't (modal future)
- can't (modal ability)
- **Teaching Point:** Modals express possibility, ability, permission

### 3. Tenses (Multiple scenes)
- Present simple: "I don't like"
- Past simple: "I knew"
- Present continuous: "We're coming"
- Future: "I won't stop," "She's gonna be"

### 4. Phrasal Verbs (2 scenes)
- hang on, hold on
- **Teaching Point:** Phrasal verbs are crucial for natural English

### 5. Homophones & Near-Homophones (4 scenes)
- trail/tail/train
- happy/heavy
- nose/road
- you're/your
- **Teaching Point:** Listening discrimination is key!

## Practice Tips

### For Listening Mode:
- Focus on contractions (they sound different!)
- Listen for the stress in sentences
- Watch out for homophones

### For Speaking Mode:
- Practice contractions naturally
- Don't over-pronounce
- Link words together (gonna, wanna, you're)

### For Reading Mode:
- Click on contractions to understand full forms
- Study the grammar patterns
- Compare formal vs. informal language

### For Writing Mode:
- Remember: contractions are ONE word
- Practice common patterns
- Don't forget apostrophes!
